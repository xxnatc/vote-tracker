The Many Deaths
of Sean Bean

Actor Sean Bean has been dying over and over in movies and TV shows for 20 years. So much so that he became known as the "walking spoiler" and spurred the #DontKillSeanBean campaign. According to this article on Nerdist, Bean holds the record of highest on-screen death percentage at 0.32 death/film, although the highest number of death belongs to John Hurt.
